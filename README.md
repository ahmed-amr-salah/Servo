# About This Project:

Servo is a robot-based on-campus food delivery system. It combines advanced robotics and seamless indoor navigation to bring delicious meals directly to your location. With Servo, you can say goodbye to long lines and rushing to find a nearby eatery, as your favorite meals are just a few taps away.
